An existing product

My time at Developerhub was the first time I joined and existing product, working in a number of different projects: Improving the navigation on mobile, redesign of the sign up/ log in page (check it out ), history view feature, the creation of illustrations for its 404 and 403 page and a number of promotional images.

I worked in close collaboration with the developer team following a Kanban methodology, both improving existing features and flows and designing new ones to be implemented on the weekly update.

Research

Giving the short period of time of which the hackathon was taking place, DePaul carried out an extensive research consisting of:

  1. Interview preparation and planning
  2. Interviews with Depaul staff
  3. Interview with young people who are receiving support from Depaul
  4. Desktop research
  5. Insights synthesis
  6. Identified Opportunity Areas and defined ‘How Might We

Every participant received a pdf document containing insights and areas of opportunity:

  • Life projects
  • Help and support
  • Coaching opperations
  • Housing support
  • Reporting and feedback collection
