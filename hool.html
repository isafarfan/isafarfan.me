Hool

Brand identity and promotional images

Role
  • Designer
Tools
  • Drawing
  • Illustrator
  • Photoshop
Deliverables
  • Logo Design
  • Promotional posters and flyers
Timeline
  • 1 Month

I was asked to create a new name brand identity for Hool, a new concept of parties in East London that unites industrial techno music and art. Behind this project were the founders on East Sunrise Party, a couple of promoters that are quite successful with their late night parties in London.

When I first approached this project I knew nothing about industrial techno, it was after a set of meetings with the founders of the project and some of the dj participating in the party that I could starting developing a solution to deliver a product that is distinguishable by those who are part of the movement.

Initially working to a tight one month deadline I was to develop a logo and a brand identity that separate this party from the other nights happening in East London

The stakeholders that a wide mix of inspiration that had to be united in an unified concept ranking from mesopotamian deities, sacred geometry, japanese calligraphy and urban architecture. The colour palette in anything related to industrial techno is reduced to exclusively black and white.
