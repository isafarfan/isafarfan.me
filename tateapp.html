The task

To observe visitors of the Tate Modern gallery in London and identify issues in their interactions with the gallery

To create a design plan that eventualy creates a solution to said issues

The research phase of this project consisted in shadowing different kinds of personas through the gallery and note down if there were any issues

Did they had any issues? Did the found what the look for? Did they seem frustrated?

The realization of a quick survey to visitors to know more about their experience after the visit
