About me

I am a UX Designer with a background as a Graphic Artist and Illustrator based in London.

In addition to visual design I have extensive experience in service and hospitality industry, I love observing and thinking about how people behave and relate to their medium.

I'm passionate about creating meaningful work with relation to nature, sustainability and equality.

I see every project I work on as a partnership with my clients, working closely with them to make sure I understand their goals and vision.
