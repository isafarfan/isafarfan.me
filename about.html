About me

I am a Product Designer based in London.

I love attending meetups and conferences, reading, and listening to podcasts related to UX, Growth Hacking and Design in general.

I'm passionate about creating meaningful work with relation to nature, sustainability and equality.

When not at work you would likely find me freelancing as a sign writter, cooking a vegan meal or having a ridiculously long walk

Every project I work on is a partnership with my clients, working closely with them to make sure I understand their goals and vision.
